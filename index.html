About Me

Newman Yange is an aspiring business owner, rapper, and creative entrepreneur. As the founder of NYNTH WORLD, he embodies resilience, ambition, and legacy. Through music, branding, and storytelling, he continues to push boundaries, inspiring others to rise above challenges and stay ahead.

Early Life

Newman Yange was born on July 15, 2007 in Nigeria. Growing up, he developed a passion for music, creativity, and entrepreneurship. Raised in a modest environment, he faced challenges that shaped his resilience and ambition. At a young age, Newman discovered an interest in rap and video editing, honing his skills through self-directed learning.

By his teenage years just age 15, he had already started building his brand, NYNTH WORLD, as a statement of perseverance and self-worth. During high school, he explored various creative and business ventures, from music production to fashion design. His dedication to his craft led him to collaborate with notable figures, including Forex trader Jeffrey Benson. Balancing school, music, and entrepreneurship, Newman remains committed to pushing boundaries and building a lasting legacy.

NYNTH World is more than a clothing brand—it's a movement. Combining urban aesthetics with premium craftsmanship, it represents Newman's vision of style and substance. At its core, NYNTH World is about inclusivity, creativity, and community.

One of Our Proudest Moments

NYNTH WORLD was a key part of a recent pop-up side event, bringing people together to celebrate creativity and inclusivity. While we weren’t the main event organizers, we played a major role in shaping the experience, adding our own touch to the atmosphere. The event was a testament to our mission: to inspire, connect, and uplift through art and fashion. It also led to a complete sell-out of our Truqha 9 Collection, marking a significant step toward our bigger vision.

A Brand for Everyone

At NYNTH World, we believe in including everyone in our journey—especially children. By involving them in our art and creative process, we aim to inspire the next generation of dreamers and creators. This inclusivity is what makes NYNTH World more than just a clothing brand—it’s a movement that empowers people to be part of something greater.

Meeting Jeffrey Benson

Newman Yange had the privilege of meeting Jeffrey Benson, a renowned figure in the world of finance and entrepreneurship. Their conversation revolved around innovation, leadership, and the future of global markets.

Member of Spyder Academy

In 2023, Newman Yange crossed paths with Michael Spyder, a visionary trader and philanthropist. What began as a meeting of minds has since evolved into a transformative mentorship. Michael’s strategic brilliance and dedication to giving back have deeply influenced Newman’s journey.

As a mentor, Michael has not only shared invaluable insights into trading and entrepreneurship but also inspired Newman to weave philanthropy into his work. Their connection is a testament to the power of shared values and the impact of meaningful relationships.

Through this partnership, Newman continues to grow, driven by Michael’s unwavering support and the belief that true success lies in uplifting others.
